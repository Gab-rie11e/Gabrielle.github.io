Final Finatasy XVI is set in the world of Valisthea, which is divided among six powerful realms, each possessing a Mothercrystal that grants them magical aether. The story follows Clive Rosfield, the protagonist who embarks on a quest for revenge after a tragedy befalls his family. Clive's younger brother, Joshua, is the Dominant of the Phoenix, but chaos ensues when the Dark Eikon, Ifrit, appears.

The story begins with Prince Noctis Lucis Caelum the heir to the throne as he sets out on a road trip with his three close friends and protectors—Gladiolus, Ignis, and Prompto—towards his wedding with Lady Lunafreya Nox Fleuret. Their journey is abruptly interrupted when they receive news that their homeland has been invaded and taken over by the Niflheim Empire, and that Noctis's father, King Regis, has been killed.

The story follows a group of individuals who become entangled in a conflict between these two worlds after being branded as l'Cie—servants of god-like beings called fal'Cie—with a mysterious and often deadly mission to complete. The protagonist, Lightning, along with her companions Snow, Sazh, Vanille, Fang, and Hope, are each given a Focus, a task they must fulfill, though the nature of their mission remains unclear. Failure to complete their Focus will turn them into monstrous Cie'th, while success turns them into crystal, granting them eternal life.
