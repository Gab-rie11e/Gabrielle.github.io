About

Final Fantasy VII Remake Intergrade is an enhanced version of the original award-winning Final Fntasy VII which was released in 1997.
Final Fantasy was well recieved by critics and gamers, praising its graphics, gameplay, story, new content, and music. The new remake
of the game boasts new-gen graphics, additions in the main story, new side quests and game modes to try, as well as new soundtracks.

The game being considered as one of the greatest games of all time has earned yet again an award from The Game Awards for having
Best Score/Music as well as recieving Best Role-Playing game of the year in 2020.
